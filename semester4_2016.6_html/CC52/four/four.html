Paper-making

Paper-making was known as one of the four great inventions of ancient China, is the spread of the human culture great inventions. It was said that by China by CAI lun in eastern han dynasty era (63-121) by the invention, but also has the archaeological evidence shows that papermaking already exist, CAI lun is the important development to improve papermaking, make the paper have higher success rate, a lower cost.




Compass

Compass is also called ones, is the main component of a mounted on the shaft needle, needle in natural is free to rotate under the action of the geomagnetic field and keep on the tangent direction of the magnetic meridian, magnetic North Pole to the geographic North Pole, the performance can be used to identify the direction. Commonly used in navigation, geodesy, travel and military, etc. Physical direction on the invention of the compass is composed of trilogy: SiNa, needle and a compass. They all belong to the invention of the China.




Power

Gunpowder is one of the four great inventions of China. Is under the influence of the appropriate external energy, able to quickly and regular combustion, and generate a large amount of high temperature gas. Mainly used as bullets, shells on military propellant and the propellant of rocket, missile and other drive energy, is an important part of ammunition. One a outstanding achievements in the history of civilization.
